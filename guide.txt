Откройте файл fabric-example-mod-1.21/src/client/java/com/example/ExampleMod.java в блокноте, поменяйте discord webhook here на свой, откройте CMD, напишите cd "путь до fabric-example-mod-1.21"

Eng:
Open fabric-example-mod-1.21/src/client/java/com/example/ExampleMod.java in Notepad, replace the (discord webhook here) with your own, open CMD, and type cd "path to fabric-example-mod-1.21"
And type: 
gradlew clean
gradlew build
Wait until it's finished, and the finished mod will be in build/libs
